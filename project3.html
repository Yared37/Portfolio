Project Details

Agricultural Census Platform

Designed data collection tools for Ethiopia's national agricultural census covering 400,000 households.

Full-stack Data Engineering Impact

Mission

Support Ethiopia's Ministry of Agriculture in collecting comprehensive agricultural data from 400,000 rural households across the country. This data is crucial for national policy decisions and agricultural development planning.

Technical Implementation

The platform was designed to work in challenging rural environments:

  • • Built offline-first data collection mobile apps
  • • Designed automatic data validation and cleaning pipelines
  • • Created training materials for 1,000+ field enumerators
  • • Implemented sync mechanisms for low-connectivity environments

Development Stages

Stage 1: Field Research

Conducted extensive field research to understand the challenges of data collection in rural Ethiopia. This included visiting remote areas and understanding the technological constraints.

Stage 2: App Development

Developed mobile applications that work offline and sync when connectivity is available. The apps needed to be intuitive for first-time smartphone users and handle multiple local languages.

Stage 3: Training Program

Created comprehensive training materials and conducted workshops for over 1,000 field enumerators. This included both technical training and cultural sensitivity training for working with rural communities.

Stage 4: Data Processing

Built robust data processing pipelines that could handle the massive volume of data while ensuring quality and consistency. Implemented automatic validation and cleaning algorithms.

Challenges Solved

  • • Handling manual entry errors with smart validation
  • • Working in areas with limited or no internet connectivity
  • • Processing and aggregating data from diverse sources
  • • Training non-technical users on digital tools

Outcome

Successfully contributed to national policy decisions with accurate, timely agricultural data. The platform enabled the collection of comprehensive agricultural data that informed national development strategies and agricultural policies.
